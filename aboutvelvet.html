About Velvet Crust Cakes

Our Story

Velvet Crust Cakes was born from a simple passion: creating desserts that feel like a hug in every bite. Our founder, Maria Santos, started baking in her small kitchen, experimenting with flavors and designs, and turning her love for sweetness into an artisanal bakery loved by the community. Every cake we craft is made with fresh, high-quality ingredients and a pinch of love.
